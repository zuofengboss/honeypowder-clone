Honey Powder 蜂蜜干粉

Honey Powder is skillfully made with high-quality liquid honey as its main component, combined with a food-grade starch carrier, utilizing drum drying technology to achieve a uniform powdered form. As a natural food ingredient, honey powder provides an authentic and pure honey taste to numerous food applications and offers a combination of benefits including nutrition, safety, ease of use, and improvements to product characteristics (such as texture, moisture retention, and color).

蜂蜜干粉是以优质液体蜂蜜为核心原料，根据不同应用需求辅以特定食用淀粉作为载体，采用先进滚筒干燥工艺精制而成的粉末状蜂蜜制品。作为源自天然的食品配料，蜂蜜干粉为多种食品行业应用提供了自然真实的味觉享受。它同时集营养健康、安全天然、功能效果、使用方便等若干优势于一身。

Why should the food industry choose honey powder?

In industrial production, traditional liquid honey often presents challenges such as handling difficulties, compromised food safety assurance, inconsistent quality stability, and limited functional efficacy. Honey powder was developed to overcome these limitations. Its successful development has not only significantly expanded the depth and breadth of natural honey's applications within the food industry but has also fundamentally improved operational convenience and enhanced product hygiene and safety.

What makes honey powder unique is that its preparation process (particularly heat drying) promotes the Maillard reaction between honey's endogenous sugars (like fructose and glucose) and substances such as amino acids. This, in turn, creates special functional attributes and a richer flavor profile not found in liquid honey.

Leveraging its high honey content and notable functional characteristics, "Bao Hetang" brand honey powder exhibits outstanding versatility and application value. Typical applications include, but are not limited to:

  • Baking Sector: Bread, cakes, mooncakes (crusts and fillings), baking premixes.
  • Seasonings and Fillings: Various meat products, flavors and spices, dusting/coating powders, peanut butter, meat fillings for steamed buns.
  • Dairy and Frozen Beverages: Non-dairy creamer, non-dairy whipped topping, ice cream, milk tablets, flavored liquid milk, milk-based beverages.
  • Snack Foods and Health Foods: Popcorn and other puffed snacks, multigrain beverages, soybean powder, rice flakes, mung bean cakes, health supplement fillers.
  • Special Applications: Certain betel nut products, etc.
