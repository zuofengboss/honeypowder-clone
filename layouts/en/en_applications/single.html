Applications of Honey Powder Products

蜂蜜干粉系列产品的应用

Bread and Cakes

Effectively improves product texture and imparts a superior mouthfeel; enhances and enriches product aroma, promoting the development of an appealing color; helps keep products moist and soft. It can also promote dough fermentation, inhibit or mask undesirable notes, aid in easy mold release, and contribute to extending the overall shelf life of products.

Frozen Dough

Honey powder plays a crucial role as an improver in frozen dough technology by protecting yeast and reducing ice crystal formation. It significantly enhances the stability of dough throughout the freezing, storage, and thawing processes, as well as the quality of the final baked product.

Meat Products

In meat product processing, honey powder is suitable for applications such as prepared meats, various types of sausages, and barbecue products. It imparts and stabilizes product color, enhances the meat's inherent aroma, harmonizes the flavors of various spices and seasonings, while also effectively inhibiting and masking undesirable flavors.

Biscuit/Cookie Products

It significantly optimizes the texture, flavor, appearance, and shelf life of biscuits/cookies, leading to an overall enhancement of their quality. In the production of mooncakes, honey powder acts as a functional natural ingredient, capable of positively enhancing product quality in numerous aspects.

Seasoning Products

In various types of seasonings, the addition of honey powder helps to better reveal the natural aromas of different ingredients, leading to a more harmonious and pure overall flavor profile. At the same time, it aids in reinforcing and accentuating the seasoning's predetermined primary flavor profile.

Ice Cream and Dairy Products

When used in ice cream products, it skillfully optimizes and blends the overall flavor profile, allowing the milky base and primary aromas to be expressed more authentically, with enhanced roundness and naturalness. At the same time, it significantly improves the smooth and delicate texture of the ice cream.

Detailed Applications

Bread Product Applications

(1) Optimization of Dough Properties: Effectively softens the gluten structure, significantly enhancing dough elasticity and extensibility. Helps increase product specific volume.

(2) Anti-staling: By retarding starch retrogradation, it effectively slows down bread staling, extending product softness and shelf life.

(3) Texture and Mouthfeel Improvement: Promotes good dough fermentation, resulting in a finer and more uniform internal crumb structure.

(4) Flavor Modification: Skillfully balances and corrects the product's primary flavor profile. Even towards the middle and later stages of its shelf life, it helps the product maintain its pure, natural flavor.

(5) Moisture Retention: Possesses excellent water-holding capabilities, significantly enhancing the dough's water retention rate.

(6) Shelf Life Extension: By effectively reducing the product's water activity, it can significantly extend the product's shelf life.

Suggested Dosage: 1.0% to 6.0% based on total flour weight

Biscuit Product Applications

(1) Maintaining Crispness: Effectively reduces the final water activity of biscuits, which is crucial for maintaining their ideal crisp or crunchy texture.

(2) Flavor Optimization: Modifies and enhances the overall flavor of biscuits, masking or balancing undesirable odors that may arise from other formula ingredients.

(3) Promotion of Ideal Color: During baking, helps promote Maillard and caramelization reactions, enabling the biscuit surface to develop a uniform, appealing golden-yellow hue.

(4) Improved Shaping and Appearance: Positively influences dough extensibility and plasticity, helping biscuits achieve sharper imprinted patterns and more defined edges.

Suggested Dosage: 1.0% to 1.3% based on flour weight

Applications in Various Cake Products

(1) Optimization of Structure: Effectively improves the internal structure of cakes, enhancing batter stability and structural support. Results in a fuller, more upright finished cake.

(2) Enhancement of Flavor Complexity: Naturally balances and enhances the overall flavor of the cake, rendering its sweetness milder and its aroma richer.

(3) Moisturization and Shelf Life: Leveraging its excellent moisturizing properties, it helps maintain the cake's moistness and soft texture for an extended period.

(4) Promotion of Ideal Color: Helps cakes develop a uniform, appealing golden-yellow or caramel color during baking.

Suggested Dosage: 0.8% to 1.2% of total cake formula weight

Applications in Certain Beverages

The application of honey powder significantly enhances the flavor complexity and authenticity of various liquid milk and cold beverage products. In products featuring pure milk, peanut, or malt flavors, it effectively mellows and enriches their original aromas.

Furthermore, in cold beverage/frozen treat applications, honey powder promotes rapid surface drying and effectively inhibits ice crystal formation, thereby imparting a finer texture and a chewier mouthfeel, while also significantly enhancing melt resistance.

Suggested Dosage: 0.2% to 1.0%

Meat Product Applications

When applied to meat products, honey powder offers multifaceted benefits including nutritional value, safety, hygiene, and ease of use, significantly enhancing product quality. It not only improves meat tenderness and chewiness but also aids in developing and retaining product color. In terms of flavor, it effectively harmonizes various spices and seasonings, lending the product a full-bodied, natural, and lasting flavor profile, while skillfully masking potential off-notes and reducing greasiness.

Suggested Dosage: 2.0% to 3.0% of total meat weight

Frozen Dough Product Applications

(1) Optimization of Rheological Properties: Effectively improves the rheological properties of frozen dough, helping to protect the integrity of the gluten network structure, significantly reducing protein damage caused by ice crystal formation during rapid freezing and subsequent freeze-thaw cycles.

(2) Enhancement of Thaw Stability: Honey powder enhances the structural strength of frozen dough, helping to inhibit dough structure deterioration during slow thawing.

(3) Strengthening the Gluten Network: Honey powder can form more stable bound water with the moisture in the dough, providing yeast with better survival space and a protective barrier in harsh freezing environments.

Suggested Dosage: 3.0% to 6.0% based on flour weight (up to 10% for specific products)
