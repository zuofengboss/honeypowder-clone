About Yancheng Huamei Honey Products Co., Ltd.

Our Factory at a Glance

Yancheng Huamei Honey Products Co., Ltd., founded in 1997, is currently located at No. 63 Xihuan Road, Yancheng City.

We are a professional honey product supplier integrating production, processing, sales, and export. Specializing in natural honey and honey powder, and leveraging nearly three decades of deep industry experience, we are consistently committed to providing global customers with natural, pure honey products of excellent quality.

Currently, we possess a strong annual production capacity of 8,000 metric tons of natural honey and 4,000 metric tons of honey powder, enabling us to reliably meet large-scale order demands.

Recognized for its technological innovation, the Honey Powder project secured a National Invention Patent, won the Gold Medal at the 6th International Invention Exposition, and furthermore, has achieved widespread market acceptance.

盐城市花美蜂蜜制品有限公司始创于1997年，现坐落于盐城市西环路63号。是一家集生产、加工、销售及出口于一体的专业蜂蜜产品供应商。我们主营天然蜂蜜与蜂蜜干粉，凭借三十余年的行业深耕，始终致力于为全球客户提供品质卓越、天然纯正的蜂蜜产品。目前，公司具备年产8000吨天然蜂蜜和4000吨蜂蜜干粉的强大生产能力，能稳定满足大规模订单需求。

凭借其技术创新性，蜂蜜干粉项目不仅已获得国家发明专利授权，并斩获第六届国际发明博览会金奖，更得到了市场的高度认可，先后被列入江苏省"星火计划"与国家科技部"国家农业科技成果转化项目"序列。
