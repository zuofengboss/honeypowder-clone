Honey Powder
Pure & Wholesome, Savor True Flavor.

健康无添加，醇享真滋味

Honey Powder is a powdered product meticulously refined through a drum drying process, using natural liquid honey as its core raw material and food-grade starch—selected according to specific application needs—as a carrier. It not only imparts an authentic and natural flavor to food products but also, by virtue of its convenient powder form, exhibits unique advantages in improving product texture, enhancing processing suitability, and extending shelf life, thereby offering the food industry an innovative natural sweetening solution.

About Honey

Honey

Honey is a natural sweet substance that bees produce by collecting plant nectar or honeydew, combining it with their own secretions (primarily enzymes), and allowing it to ripen through a thorough process. Scientific analysis shows that honey contains over 180 different substances; it primarily consists of fructose and glucose (together accounting for more than 70%), while also including various vitamins, minerals, amino acids, and enzymes as trace active compounds.
